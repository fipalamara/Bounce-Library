Introduction

This site is a collection of examples of bounce in computing and other contexts.

The aim of the collection is not completeness or comprehensiveness.

Instead the idea is to draw together many different kinds of computer bounce
in order to think about how bounce operates as a cultural technique.
Bounce is a kind of interaction. It is one possible outcome from collision, an outcome
which serves to constitute and confirm identity. If things don't bounce they break, or fuse, or orbit,
or pass through each other.The primary focus of this site is examples of how bounce is and has been
programmed in computing. Bounce programs are the common sense of computer graphics...
